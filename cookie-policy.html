What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your browser and remember some of your preferences, like language, items in your cart, or consent choices. Cookies are essential to enhance your online experience and allow us to analyze how you use our site.

Types of cookies used

  • Technical and necessary cookies: Essential for the correct functioning of the website. They allow you to navigate pages and use key features, such as access to private areas or cart management. Without these cookies, some parts of the site might not work properly.
  • Performance cookies: These cookies collect anonymous and aggregated information about how visitors use the site, which pages are most visited, and if errors occur. This helps us improve the quality of the site and offer more relevant content.
  • Profiling and marketing cookies: We use third-party cookies such as Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and other advertising services to provide personalized ads and track the effectiveness of campaigns. These cookies also collect information about your browsing on other sites.

Consent management

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via the banner shown on first visit or by adjusting your browser settings. You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential ones, or customize your choices.
